ELECT-ERA

The whimsical work from the professors and students of the department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, CIT, is the nation-wide technical engineering symposium, Elect-Era. The latter is focussing on the masses of youth, primarily engineering aspirants. Elect-era is collaborated with industrial experts as well as academic experts, in order to plant a core fundamental to the amazing participants. Elect-era helps the players to achieve their maximum academic intelligence and goals.
